Frequently Asked Questions

How early should I arrive?

We recommend arriving 10-15 minutes prior to your appointment time. This allows you to fill out any necessary forms and start relaxing before your session begins.

Can I get a massage if I have an injury?

It depends on the injury. Please inform us when booking and tell your therapist before the session. In many cases, massage helps recovery, but we may need to avoid specific areas.

What is your cancellation policy?

We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee.

Is parking available?

Yes! We have a dedicated parking lot right in front of the store, so parking is free and convenient.
